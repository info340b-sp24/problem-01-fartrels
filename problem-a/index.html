Yoichi Isagi

Yoichi Isagi is the main character of hit manga, now turned anime, Blue Lock . In this story he and other high school soccer players are locked in a facility created by the Japan National Football Team that aims to develop the ultimate striker in order to win the World Cup. There, Isagi learns what it means to be a true striker and realizes his special abilities that aid him on the football pitch to score goals. His special ability is his high spacial awareness of what's happening on the field, helping him get in the right spots to score goals himself or assist his teammates in scoring.
